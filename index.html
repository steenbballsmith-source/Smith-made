How it works

From our shop to your Saturday

  1. Pick your pieces

    Browse the collection, then tell us your date, your venue, and what caught your eye. We’ll confirm the date is free and send a simple quote.

  2. Book it with 50%

    Your deposit covers the materials and locks your date on our calendar. The moment it’s paid, your build goes on the bench.

  3. We deliver & style

    We bring everything to the venue, set each piece where it belongs, and have it letter‑perfect before your first guest arrives.

  4. Last dance, we load out

    Rentals go home with us after the reception — you never lift a thing. Keepsake pieces go home with you, forever.

Rent‑to‑own: our signature offer

Any eligible rental can be yours to keep. Half of what you paid in rent comes off the purchase price. Rent the arched welcome sign for $95, fall in love with it over the weekend, and take it home for $203 instead of $250. No pressure, no expiration talk at the reception — just tell us before pickup.

    About

    Handbuilt in Greenville

    Smith Made is a small woodworking shop in Greenville, South Carolina, run by Steen Smith. We build carpenter-scale wedding signage — the kind of pieces a calligraphy studio can’t make and a party-rental warehouse doesn’t carry: seven-foot arched walls, seating charts that stand on their own, a champagne wall, a rolling bar.

    The idea is simple. Couples here spend real money making their wedding beautiful, and the biggest, most photographed pieces shouldn’t have to be bought, shipped, or thrown away. So we rent them — delivered, styled, and picked up — and the pieces made to be yours forever, we engrave and you keep.

    Everything is built new to order in the shop. Nothing goes on the bench until it’s booked, which is why every piece arrives looking like it was made for your day. It was.

    Questions

    Asked and answered

    How far do you deliver?

    Anywhere in the Upstate. Delivery, setup, and pickup is a flat $75 within 25 miles of Greenville, then $2 per mile beyond — which puts Spartanburg, Anderson, Clemson, and Asheville, NC all comfortably in range.

    How far ahead should we book?

    Weddings happen on Saturdays, and we only take what one shop can deliver and style well in a day. Spring and fall Saturdays go first — if you have your date, it’s worth checking now. The 50% deposit is what locks it.

    Can we personalize a rental piece?

    Yes — that’s the design. Your names and date go on in a removable layer (lettering, an engraved plaque, or a chalkboard insert), so the piece arrives personalized to you. Permanent, deep-engraved personalization is what our keepsake line is for.

    What happens if something gets damaged?

    Rentals carry a refundable damage deposit — $100 or 25% of the rental price — returned after pickup inspection. Normal wear is our problem, not yours. We also carry general liability insurance and can send a certificate to your venue.

    How exactly does rent‑to‑own work?

    Half of what you paid in rent counts toward the purchase price, capped at one event’s rent. Example: the arched welcome sign rents for $95 and sells for $250 — keep it after your wedding for $250 − $47 = $203. Just tell us before we load out.

    Do you build things that aren’t for weddings?

    Happily. Anniversary and “established” signs, housewarming pieces, holiday commissions — winter is build season in the shop. Use the inquiry form and tell us what you’re imagining.
