## Three build-video scripts (film vertical; these are the whole funnel)

**Script 1 — "Part 1" (15–25s).** Hook line on screen: *"Building a
7-foot wedding arch from scratch — Part 1."* Shots: raw lumber on the
bench → planing/sanding close-up (dust in the light) → laying out the
arch curve → cutting it. End card: "Part 2 tomorrow." Caption: "Every
piece starts as boards in a Greenville shop. Part 2 tomorrow. 🪵"
**Script 2 — "Part 2" (15–25s).** Hook: *"The part where it gets a
name."* Shots: lettering being applied/carved → finish coat going on →
hardware. End card: "Reveal next." Caption: "Names on. Finish on.
Tomorrow she stands up for the first time."
**Script 3 — "Reveal" (10–20s).** Hook: *"3 weeks ago this was a pile
of boards."* Shots: loading the truck → carrying into venue/yard →
slow pan of the finished piece styled with flowers at golden hour.
Caption: "Handbuilt in Greenville. Rent it for your weekend or keep it
forever — link in bio. Which piece should we build next?"

Repost every video to Instagram Reels, TikTok, Facebook, and YouTube
Shorts — same file, four audiences, zero extra work.

## When ads day comes (after real photos + first reviews)

Boost the best-performing build Reel from Instagram: goal "messages" or
"website visits" → audience: Engaged, 25 miles around Greenville, ages
22–40 → $5–10/day → 7 days → then judge ONLY by inquiries received.
If a $70 test brings 2+ real inquiries, scale slowly. If not, back to
organic — the venue channel converts better per dollar at this stage.
