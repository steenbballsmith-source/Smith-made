# Smith Made — Marketing, Outreach & Lead Playbook

The complete plan to get Smith Made in front of Upstate couples, in the
order that costs the least and converts the most. Built from the business
plan: relationships and search beat paid directories at launch.

**The funnel, in one line:** couples see a piece (Marketplace, Instagram,
Google) → land on the website → inquire or pay the $50 date hold → Square
invoice for the 50% deposit → build, deliver, review, repeat.

Site: https://steenbballsmith-source.github.io/Smith-made/

  décor · Greenville, SC · rent it or keep it forever · check your date ↓"
  + site link.
- **3–4 posts/week.** Rotation: build process clip (these outperform

  Reels/TikTok. The shot list is in the handoff §11: planing close-ups,

  (they're all on the hit list above).
- Pinterest: same content, boards per category ("Wood wedding welcome
  signs," "Seating chart walls," "Ceremony arches") — Pinterest traffic
  compounds for years.

## 5. Etsy (keepsakes only, month 2)

List The Family Round + engraved welcome signs (shippable sizes only).
Never list rentals. This is winter cash flow.

## 6. Paid ads — later, deliberately

- **Skip The Knot/WeddingWire now** ($200–450/mo, 12-month lock, shared
  leads). Revisit at month 6 with data.
- Once you have 3+ real photos: boost the best Reel $5–10/day, audience
  "Engaged," 25 miles around Greenville. That's the whole paid plan
  until bookings prove more.
- Bridal show booth ($300–800) once the fleet exists — book the next
  Greenville show when you have 4+ physical pieces.

## 7. The 30-day execution calendar

**Week 1:** GBP created + verified · date-hold link live on site ·
2 Marketplace listings up · IG/Pinterest accounts live · first 3 venue
emails (Riverain, Sitton Hill, The Hollow).
**Week 2:** renew listings · 3 more venue emails · 2 planner coffee asks ·
first build-process Reel · GBP post #2 · join 3 FB groups.
**Week 3:** renew · 3 venue emails · visit 2 venues in person with a
one-pager · GBP post #3 · founder-batch announcement post (5 slots, 20%
off, photos+review).
**Week 4:** renew · follow-ups on all non-replies · book styled shoot
with the warmest venue+photographer pair · review month: what produced
inquiries? Double down there in month 2.

**Budget month 1: $0–$50** (everything above is free; $50 buffer for a
boosted post if a Reel takes off). The startup money stays for insurance,
materials, and easels — exactly per the business plan.

## 8. What success looks like

- Month 1: GBP verified, 10+ venue/planner contacts made, 2 listings
  renewing weekly, 5–10 inquiries, first date holds paid.
- Month 3: preferred-vendor at 3+ venues, 15+ GBP photos, first 5
  reviews, founder batch sold out.
- Month 6: reviews ≥15, raise prices toward the premium column, decide
  on The Knot with real cost-per-lead data.
